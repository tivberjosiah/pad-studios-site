Who we are

About PAD Studios

We are a small Roblox game studio at the start of our journey. Our focus is systems that feel smooth, GUIs that stay readable, and experiences that friends can grind together for hours.

Our vision

We want to build games that reward smart decisions, teamwork, and commitment. No pay-to-win walls. No lazy design. If a feature goes in, it earns its spot.

  • Deep, looping gameplay that stays fun late game
  • Clean cartoony visuals that feel like Roblox but sharper
  • Systems you can learn, break, and master
  • Fair monetization that respects players
Engine: Roblox Studio Core skills: Lua scripting, building, UI design

What we focus on

Every project starts with core loops and player experience. If the moment to moment gameplay feels good, art and effects can level it up from there.

  • Efficient scripting and server logic
  • Queue, shop, and inventory systems
  • Intuitive UI for mobile and PC
  • Smooth progression and rewards

What is next

Studio roadmap

Plans can shift as we learn from testing, but this gives a rough idea of where PAD Studios is heading.

Timeline

From scripting and building to live testing with players, this is how we plan to grow PAD Studios step by step.

Prototype core systems
Lock in queue, shop, and progression systems that feel good for both solo players and parties.
Closed testing
Invite early testers to break our systems, find bugs, and help shape balance and pacing.
Visual polish
Refine maps, lighting, sound, and UI so everything feels clean, readable, and cartoony in the best way.
Public launch
Release our first main title, listen hard to feedback, and keep shipping updates that improve the core loop.
